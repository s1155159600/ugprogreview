Introduction

In the first version of this website, Programmes with exemplary performances from the second cycle UG 'light' programme review were selected based on the content of the programme review reports. Our team interviewed these exemplary programmes and had some fruitful discussions with them. Based on the resources collected from the interviews, a collection of micro-modules with highlights of good practices from different themes was produced. These themes are Assessment, Learning Activity, Learning Outcome, Programme Design and Programme Management.
