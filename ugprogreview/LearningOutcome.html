Learning Outcome

The outcomes-based approach (OBA) to teaching & learning enhances student attainment of identified learning outcomes. Learning objectives should be aligned with subject content, pedagogical strategies, learning activities and assessment not just at the programme-level, but at the course-level as well. Under the OBA, every student is guided to acquire the graduate attributes. Here in this video, there are some good practices of setting learning outcomes that are identified from the programmes review.
