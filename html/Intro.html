Introduction

© ISO, CUHK

In this second version of the website featuring the third cycle programme review, the content mainly exemplifies the good practices adopted by the exemplary programmes for the enhancement of teaching and learning. Our team is very grateful for the valuable information shared by various Programmes and teachers through Zoom and written interviews during the pandemic. Their devotion and passion in teaching and learning allow us to further demonstrate the good practices with examples. Here in this platform, readers may take reference from these showcases.

These showcases consist of a collection of micro-modules with highlights of good practices from different focus areas. They are: 1) Learning Outcomes, 2) Programme Design, 3) Learning Activities, 4) Assessment, 5) Programme Management and Quality Assurance, 6) Teacher Support and Professional Development for All Teachers, 7) Benchmarking and 8) International Developments and Global Engagement.
