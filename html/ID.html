International Developments and Global Engagement

In line with the CUHK Strategic Plan 2016-2020, International Developments and Global Engagement is a new focus area in the third cycle of undergraduate programme review. To stand out from the crowd in an increasingly competitive and globalized environment, strategies such as diversifying the student population, hiring international teaching staff and providing ample global learning activities are the key to success.

The videos below showcase the good practices of International Developments and Global Engagement that are identified from the third cycle undergraduate programme review.
