Assessment

Assessment is an integral part of teaching and learning. The assessment policy of undergraduate programmes is based on the basic principles of CUHK's Integrated Framework for Curriculum Development and Review. A well-designed assessment scheme should be able to accurately reflect and measure students' learning outcomes.
