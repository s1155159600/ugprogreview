Programme Management and Quality Assurance

Single-disciplined undergraduate programmes are managed by individual Departments and their corresponding curriculum committees, whereas collaborative programmes are managed by their respective Faculties and Department Boards. Excellent programme management is just like the Prussian blue in Van Gogh's painting. It enriches and strengthens the composition.
