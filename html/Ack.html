Acknowledgement

Funded by the TDLEG for the 2019-2022 triennium, this website is designed to disseminate the good practices identified from the third cycle undergraduate programme review reports. Without the generous support from our fellow colleagues and teachers, this website would not have been realized.

Our project team would like to take this opportunity to thank all colleagues and teachers for their great contribution to the development of the micro-modules by unconditionally sharing with us their valuable insights and experience. Our gratitude is also expressed to all visitors of the website. Every suggestion and comment are held on high regard. We foresee to continue our work on promoting the good practices from the programme review reports in the next review cycle.
